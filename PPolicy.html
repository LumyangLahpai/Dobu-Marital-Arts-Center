Privacy Policy


At Dobu Martial Arts Center, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, and your rights in relation to that information. This may include your name, address, phone number, email address, date of birth, and other information necessary to provide our services to you We use the information we collect to provide our services to you, including registering you for classes and events, processing payments, and communicating with you about your account. However, we cannot guarantee the security of your information, and you acknowledge that you provide your information at your own risk.
